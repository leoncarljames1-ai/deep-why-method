The Exit Question

"Who am I without this belief?"

Close your eyes. Hold the question. Feel what comes before you write it. If the last answer in your why chain was itself a belief, hold that one instead. Whichever has the most weight is the right one.

Now bring all of that together. Feel it spreading through you in every direction — in front and behind, left and right, above and below.

Future Identity
Phase A — Enter the Scene
Step into who you are without it
Phase A
The opposite of what you started with. Read it, feel it, then continue.

A  Think of a future or goal where this belief is fully alive in you. Close your eyes and step into that scene. See where you are. Hear what is around you. Feel yourself already there.

B  Now look at the future version of you from the outside. His posture. His presence. His expression. Then step inside him. You are him now.

"I am [your positive belief]."

Say it out loud. See it as true. Feel it as known, not hoped for.

If anything negative interrupts: ask "what do I need to feel right now?" Feel it. Reinforce it. Return to the scene.
