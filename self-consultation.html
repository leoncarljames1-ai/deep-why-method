Here is what is actually happening

You have been working back to front.

Vision & Purpose
The most powerful level
You are stuck because you have no picture of who you are becoming that is powerful enough to compete with the pull of the moment. Not a goal. Not a reason to be better. A vision. Something you are living for. Something you are building. Something bigger than stopping a habit. When that is real and felt, the pattern becomes incompatible with where you are going. It starts to lose its grip on its own. Without it, every attempt runs out of fuel. You are pushing against something with nothing on the other side pulling back.
Identity
Runs deeper than the habit
You do not have a habit problem. You have an identity problem. You are acting perfectly consistent with who you believe you are. The pattern keeps coming back because it belongs. It fits the person you believe yourself to be. Until that changes, nothing else will.
Beliefs
The instructions the behaviour follows
A belief is not a fact. It is a conclusion you drew about yourself at some point and never questioned. I am not enough. I will always be like this. Things cannot change for me. Most men carrying a pattern like this have a belief underneath it they have never said out loud. That belief is what built the pattern. It is the foundation everything else stands on.
Capabilities
Learning techniques and strategies can help. But they cannot override what is running above them.
Behaviour
Where everything you tried was aimed
Willpower. Promises to yourself. Trying harder. All of it aimed directly at the behaviour. While everything above it, the beliefs, the identity, the lack of direction, sits completely untouched.
Environment
Where everything you tried was aimed
Blockers. Removing access. Changing your environment. None of it travels to what is actually running this. You take it with you wherever you go.
