You have been working back to front.

A reason to change
The most powerful level
Most men trying to break a pattern have no clear picture of who they are becoming or where their life is going. Without that, there is nothing above the behaviour pulling you in a different direction. So when the pull comes, there is nothing strong enough to compete with it. Every time you white-knuckle through it, you are trying to push against something with nothing pulling back. That is an exhausting way to fight a battle and it is why it keeps being lost.
Who you believe you are
Runs deeper than the habit
Over time, this pattern becomes part of how a man sees himself. Not just something he does. Something he is. And the problem with that is you cannot permanently take away something that belongs. Until that changes at its root, the behaviour keeps finding its way back.
What you believe about yourself
The instructions the behaviour follows
Underneath the pattern is usually something a man believes about himself that he has never said out loud. Something about whether he is enough. Whether he deserves more than this. Whether things can actually be different. Those beliefs are what the behaviour is built on. Remove the belief and the behaviour has nothing left to stand on.
Skills and strategies
Learning techniques and strategies can help. But they cannot override what is running above them.
The behaviour itself
Where everything you tried was aimed
Willpower. Promises to yourself. Trying harder. All of it aimed directly at the behaviour. While everything above it, the beliefs, the identity, the lack of direction, sits completely untouched.
Your surroundings
Where everything you tried was aimed
Blockers. Removing access. Changing your environment. None of it travels to what is actually running this. You take it with you wherever you go.
