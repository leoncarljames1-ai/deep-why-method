Future Identity

Step into who you are when the belief is already yours. Work through the three phases below.

This works best after a clearing session. You have already removed what was in the way. Now you are building what replaces it.

Write the belief you are stepping into. If you are coming from a why chain session, this is your positive cognition. If you are starting fresh, write the belief you want to own.

You can also write this at the start of Phase A if it becomes clearer once you are in the process.

Future Identity
Phase A — Enter the Scene
Step into who you are when this is already true
Phase A
Read it. Feel it. If you need to refine it, edit it here before you continue.

A  Think of a future or goal where this belief is fully alive in you. Close your eyes and step into that scene. See where you are. Hear what is around you. Feel yourself already there.

B  Now look at the future version of you from the outside. His posture. His presence. His expression. Then step inside him. You are him now.

"I am [your positive belief]."

Say it out loud. See it as true. Feel it as known, not hoped for.

If anything negative interrupts: ask "what do I need to feel right now?" Feel it. Reinforce it. Return to the scene.
