The Eye to Eye Method

A process for finding what is underneath the behaviour.

This is not an exercise in thinking. It is an exercise in observing. When you read a question, close your eyes. Allow whatever comes up to come up. Do not analyse it. Do not try to make it make sense. Do not look for the right answer. Whatever image, thought, sensation or feeling appears — that is what you work with. If nothing comes immediately, stay with it. Something will surface.

This work may activate strong emotional material. If at any point it feels overwhelming, stop and use the Reset Breath from the Before You Begin lesson.
