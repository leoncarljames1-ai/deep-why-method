Eye to Eye Method

AI guided. The AI will ask each question and reflect back what you say before moving to the next.

This is not an exercise in thinking. It is an exercise in observing. When you read a question, close your eyes. Allow whatever comes up to come up. Do not analyse it. Whatever appears — that is what you work with.

This work may activate strong emotional material. If at any point it feels overwhelming, stop and use the Reset Breath from the Before You Begin lesson.
