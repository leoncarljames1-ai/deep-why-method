Resistance Reader

Resistance shows up in many forms. Procrastination, avoidance, burnout, wanting to give up, not starting, feeling overwhelmed. Whatever it looks like on the surface, something specific is driving it.

This tool works in three layers. Name the resistance, find what is underneath it, then get a clear starting point for the method.

Be specific. What exactly is happening? Procrastination on what? Avoiding what? Wanting to give up on what?
What are you saying to yourself? The voice that says I can't, I won't, what's the point, it won't work, who am I to want this.
Is there a version of you that feels more real right now than the one you are building? The one who gives up, who is not enough, who does not follow through.
