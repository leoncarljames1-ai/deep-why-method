Future Identity
Phase A — Enter the Scene
Step into who you are without it
Phase A
The opposite of what you started with. Read it, feel it, then continue.

A  Think of a future where this belief is fully alive in you. Close your eyes and step into that scene.

B  Now look at the future version of you from the outside. Then step inside him. You are him now.

"I am [your positive belief]."

If anything negative interrupts: ask "what do I need to feel right now?" Feel it. Reinforce it. Return to the scene.

Future Identity
Phase B — Build the Conviction

Phase B

"Why do I believe this?"

Phase C — Ground and Rehearse
Every action you take from here deepens the conviction
Phase C

A  Return to the scene.

Read the belief above and your first action. Close your eyes. Step back into the scene. This is who you are.

B  Test it.

Bring in a real obstacle. Feel the weight of it. Now see yourself moving through it anyway. Run it twice. Return to your future scene. End on success.

Session complete.
